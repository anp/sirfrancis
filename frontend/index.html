Smarter, non-stereotyping, service-independent movie recommendations.

a.k.a. Movie Recommendations 7: The Suggestioning

A movie provider can only suggest movies based on what you have watched or bought using that same service. Chances are, you're using multiple services and that no single one can recommend movies based on your complete viewing habits.

Further, the recommendations of most providers are only based on what makes you like other people who use that same service, not what your individual tastes say about what you enjoy watching. We hope to change that.
